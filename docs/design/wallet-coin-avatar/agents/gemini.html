Wallet Avatar Explorations (Gemini)

Round 2 discards the generic "metal coin" paradigm to explore three distinct, material-expressive languages. Ranging from an organic fluid resin, to a dense manufactured ceramic tile, to a raw technical cipher tag. All rely purely on HTML/CSS with system fonts, avoiding network assets while maintaining sharp legibility down to 24px.

A thick, glassy squircle with deep refraction. Feels highly premium and organic, standing out from typical flat UI. Strong inner highlights maintain the dimensional pop at 24px without getting muddy.
